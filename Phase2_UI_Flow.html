The mobile app was deprioritised in Q2 following a strategic review in late January.[1] Three factors drove the decision:

1. Engineering bandwidth redirect — After three enterprise customers flagged API v2 as a blocker for renewal, engineering capacity was reallocated from mobile to the API platform.[2][3]

2. Revised mobile DAU projections — Beta cohort data showed mobile DAU tracking 40% below forecast. The PRD was updated to reflect this in Section 4.2, moving mobile to "Future Scope."[4]

3. Pricing restructuring focus — The concurrent usage-based pricing initiative required full PM and design attention for Q2.[5]
